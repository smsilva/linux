---
name: daily
description: Generate a daily standup update based on recent conversation history
---

Based on our recent conversations, generate my standup update for today:
- What I did
- What I'm going to do
- Blockers or impediments (if any)

Keep it concise. Nobody likes long standups.
Be direct and to the point.
Use bullet points for readability.
If today is Monday, include a brief summary of what was done last week. Saturdays count as last week. Sundays count as this week.
